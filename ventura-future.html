Ventura Future

(UX project, microsite)

2020

Overview

Ventura Future is a cultural organization based in Milan whose focus is to highlight and promote contemporary design and new designers. Designers are urged to apply to be part of the design exhibitions that are curated by the organization and the exhibitions are held during the annual Milan Design Week.

Poster Campaign

Ventura Future is a cultural organization based in Milan whose focus is to highlight and promote contemporary design and new designers. As Ventura Future relies on applicant participation for the exhibitions to exist, this microsite aims to incentivize prospective exhibitors to register. Past data from the 2019 event is showcased to give credibility to the organization and promote the benefits of the 2020 exhibition.
