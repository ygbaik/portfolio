Style Guide

Colours

Deep Yellow

#FED60A

A deep yellow was chosen as the primary colour for the portfolio to represent enthusiasm and energy. It's the main characteristic element of the portfolio.

Black

#000000

Black is used for the text content of the portfolio to be legible on the brighter yellow background.

Typeface

Aktiv Grotesk

Aktiv Grotesk was used to convey a sense of composure and professionalism with its swiss typography roots as being authoritative, but neutral - a good typeface to represent the diverse capabilities and flexibilities a UX designer would need.

Interactive Elements

Interactive Elements and Links become italicized when hovered.

This was done keep a consistent swiss look throughout the portfolio without having anything overpower the content.

For mobile where hover interactions aren't possible, the links and interactive elements have been labeled in other ways to indicate their being and function through text. (ie. higher hierarchy, "click here to...", grouped with other clickable elements, etc)
